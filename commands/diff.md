`diff` shows diff between 2 files

`diff -u --color=always old_file new_file`: show diff in *unified format* as used by `git diff` and use colored output

what is 'unified format'? u can refer to [here](https://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/480443/how-shall-i-understand-the-unified-format-of-diff-output) for details.
